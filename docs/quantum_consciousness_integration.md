```math
Φ = min_{B∈P} (KL(p(x_t|x_{t-1}) || ∏ᵢ p(x^i_t|x^i_{t-1})))
```

- Φ: Integrated information
- KL: Kullback-Leibler divergence

- P: Set of bipartitions

```math
C_l(ρ) = ∑_{i≠j} |ρ_{ij}|
```

- ρ: Density matrix

- C_l: l1-norm of coherence
